Phase 1: Exploring Digital Media


Project Fuego

This was our very first project in this class. We each created a short introduction of ourselves with our various backgrounds and goals we hope to achieve in this class.

New Media Webtext Experiment

For this making we used our knowledge from the first project to produce something even more thourough and varying designs. I used this opportunity to explore different font and background colors as well as playing with different picture size, alignment, and placment.

Branching or Non-linear Webtext Design

For this project we were tasked to create a non linear design. Meaning a layout that extends and changes over multiple pages. I used this opportunity to create a somewhat interactive interface that promtps users about how they are feeling. With this project I was able to explore different layouts and making sure themes and media is consistent over multiple pages.

Phase 1 Course Goals


1. Foster the creative habits of mind

So far in this course I have been able to explore a multitude of different media and approaches that have widely broadened my knowledge of different design aspects and attributes. Exploring through the numerous readings and resources availble in this class, I was able to understand the fundamentals and cruical points when it comes to making digital media and interactive platforms.

2. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

The layout and approach of this class makes it very easy to interact with peers and not only help eachother but gain knowledge from one another. We all have come into this course with different skill sets and goals in mind, but I think that is something that contributes to the beauty of this class. Numerous times have I found myself seeking help from my peers and they have been able to point me in the right direction. What is fascinating to me is seeing how people incorporate the basics of designs differently from on another and the infinte number of different projects that could be made.

3. Think deeply about craft.

So far this course has truly helped me explore my deepest thoughts and dig deep into my creative flow. With the first set of projects being somewhat variations of one another, each time I felt like I the need to dig a little deeper within myself to create something that is both meaningful but also educational or informative. I realized that while making these projects, there is truly endless posibilities and I really love that aspect of it. When you truly think about it there are so many small details, from font theme to picture placment and word allignment there are so many small things that could ultimately make or break a design.

4. Engage in sustained research and skills development.

In just a few weeks I have greatly learned about the numerous design aspects that go into making these types of projects. I think the skill I have refined the most so far is definetly using the Atom interface. Prior to taking this class I had a limited knowledge of coding especially its use for design. However in under a month I am now able to identify different types of code and know how it would be presented on a website or interface. As the course goes on I plan to dive deeply into the various resources availble and polish my design and coding skills to the fullest.

5. Learn to present your work

While I feel quite confident when presentimg my work, I believe that I have a few areas of improvment to work on. Firstly I think that I should enunciate and project my words alot more. Secondly I feel like I could definelty put much more energy into my speech delivery. I think I often have great ideas and piece them well together but the struggle comes alive when its time to demonstrate my ideas how I portrayed them
